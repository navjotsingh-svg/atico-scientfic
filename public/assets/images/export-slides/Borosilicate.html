Why Glass Grade Determines Total Cost of Ownership

Borosilicate glass (typically ~80% silica with boron trioxide) expands far less under heat than ordinary soda-lime glass. For a lab running repeated heating, autoclaving, or aggressive reagent cycles, this difference is not cosmetic — it dictates breakage rates, safety incidents, and how often a purchasing department has to re-tender for replacements.
